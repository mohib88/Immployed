About Us Page

Our application connects employers with the recently unemployed who have recovered from COVID-19 and have developed an immunity to the virus, and as such, are able to work relatively safely. With 2 million unemployment cases in Canada in April alone, Canadians are eager to work and start making money again. Our team has worked to create a safe and reliable way to start the economy once more.
